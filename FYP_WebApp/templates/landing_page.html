About Quora

Quora is a Q&A platform where users discuss various topics, including MOOCs. It provides rich, unbiased, and often detailed user-generated reviews, making it a valuable data source for sentiment analysis, free from platform-hosted review biases.

The formal, long-form responses help in understanding nuanced sentiments across different learning aspects.
